1. How to Talk to Your Cat About Gun Safety

This humorous guide takes a tongue-in-cheek look at protecting your household from feline-related firearms accidents. It provides purr-fectly absurd advice on teaching your furry friend about the dangers of laser pointers, the four rules of gun safety, and what to do if you find your cat stashing a revolver in the litter box.Where to find it: You can pick up a real copy or check out hilarious merch based on this internet-famous title via Amazon.

2. The Big Book of Lesbian Horse Stories

A brutally honest, dark comedy survival manual for tech workers. This collection of satirical essays roasts the reality of modern developer life, exploring existential terrors like "legacy code," the mystery of completely non-existent documentation, and why refactoring code is basically just therapy.

3. Let’s Pretend This Never Happened

Subtitled "A Mostly True Memoir," this laugh-out-loud comedy book details the wildly eccentric life of The Bloggess. From growing up with a taxidermist father to dealing with anxiety through hysterical, bizarre life events, it is widely praised as an incredibly funny, relatable, and sometimes slightly unhinged read.
